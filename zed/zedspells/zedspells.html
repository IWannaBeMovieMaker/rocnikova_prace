Contempt for the Weak

Cost: none

Range: 125

Passive: Zed's basic attacks against targets below 50% Health deal 6-10%
of the target's maximum Health as bonus Magic Damage.
This effect can only occur once every 10 seconds on the same target.

Razor Shuriken

Cost: 75/ 70/ 65/ 60/ 55 Energy

Range: 900

Active:Zed and his shadows throw their shurikens, dealing 80 / 115 / 150 / 185 / 220 (+1.0 per bonus attack damage)
Living Shadow: Overlapping Shadow Slashes striking the same enemy deal no additional damage
Each shuriken deals damage to every enemy hit.
Cooldown: 6 seconds

Living shadow

Cost: 40 / 35 / 30 / 25 / 20 Energy

Range: 650

Effect radius: 1950
Active: Zed and his shadows create a burst of shadow, dealing 70 / 95 / 120 / 145 / 170 (+0.8 per bonus attack damage)
physical damage to nearby enemies. Zed's slash reduces the cooldown of Living Shadow by 1 second for each enemy struck.
shadows' slashes slow enemies by 20 / 25 / 30 / 35 / 40% for 1.5 seconds.
Living Shadow: Overlapping Shadow Slashes striking the same enemy deal no additional damage
but will incur an increased slow of 30 / 37.5 / 45 / 52.5 / 60% and restore energy.
Cooldown 5 / 4.5 / 4 / 3.5 / 3 seconds

Death Mark

Cost: none

Range: 625

Active: Zed becomes untargetable and dashes to target enemy champion.
Upon arrival, he marks the target for death and spawns a living shadow at his starting position.
This shadow lasts 6 seconds and mimics Zed's abilities.
After 0.5 seconds Zed can reactivate Death Mark, causing him to switch positions with this shadow.
After 3 seconds, the mark will detonate, dealing physical damage equal to 100%
of Zed's attack damage plus 25 / 40 / 55% of damage
dealt of all magic and physical damage dealt to the target by Zed and his shadows while the mark was active.
Cooldown 120 / 90 / 60 seconds
